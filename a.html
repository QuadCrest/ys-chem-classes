     Salunkhe Classes was founded in 1982 by the vision and toil of Prof. Sudhir N Gayawal, a Mechanical Engineer from Mumbai. It is a leading Coaching Institute in Pune that provides comprehensive test preparatory services to students preparing for Medical and Engineering entrance exams. A systematized methodology was introduced that changed the very face of education in Pune for the better. A fresh chapter began in the city for entrance exam coaching with the introduction of a more professional approach to teaching and staff management.
